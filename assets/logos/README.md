Place your club logos in this folder.

Recommended naming convention (automatic by team code):

- `ala.png`, `ath.png`, `atm.png`, `bar.png`, `cel.png`
- `elc.png`, `esp.png`, `get.png`, `gir.png`, `lev.png`
- `osa.png`, `ray.png`, `mll.png`, `bet.png`, `rma.png`
- `ovi.png`, `rso.png`, `sev.png`, `val.png`, `vil.png`

You can also override logos via URL params:

- `homeLogo=https://.../home.png`
- `awayLogo=https://.../away.png`
